ABOUT US

BACKGROUND

The Nsukka Vocational Skills and Talent Competition (NVSTC) mission is to advance the effectiveness of different vocations by recognizing excellence, preparing our community for better and prosperous future, fostering innovation, sharing best practices and building networks locally and internationally.

OBJECTIVE

Allow the Nsukka talents to source from international best practices in order to go and embed the impartation from the networking and training sessions in their respective societies across Nsukka and beyond. Allow well renowned leaders to impart both their positive and challenging experiences in the different sectors. Upcoming and future talents to network and meet talents who have put Nigeria on the Worlds map, Nigeria talents who have transformed their communities, penetrated global manufactures, talents who are influencing their societies by bringing a positive economic growth. Young enthusiast should leverage from the experience of legendary talents.

VISION

Continuously identifying future capable talents to train and empower them through workshops, conferences and networking.

MISSION

To continuously empower young talents across Nsukka zone to the future, to grow partnerships across Nigeria. To accomplish its mission, Nsukka Youth Empowerment program will hold Talent competition to sieve out the best talent in our communities across the State. Each Competition will draw over 5,000 attendees from across the Nsukka and beyond including other youth project nationally.
