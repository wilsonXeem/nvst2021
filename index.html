NSUKKA TALENT AND SKILL COMPETITION AND AWARD 2021

It's time to celebrate Nsukka talents

Nsukka Vocational Skill & Talent Competition and Awards
2021

Call us: 091 5049 9394

Lets build Nsukka of our desire

The aim of this mega event is to encourage and reward skilled Nsukka youths and bring the undiscovered talents limelight.
Our mandate is to empower the participants and encourage self-reliance and productivity in Nsukka town.

HAIR STYLIST

Knowledge without practice and the right exposure is like a poisonous venom in the body. Hair stylist are amazing persons because of their ability to change people’s look just by working on their hair.

TAILOR

Nsukka vocational skills and talent competition 2021 gives you the platform to show the world what you can do with your sewing machine. Empowerment and promotions of brand is not left out.

CARPENTRY AND WOODWORK

Carpentry and woodwork is a high on demand skill. The mentality associated with this skill being as a poor man’s job is wrong. Mesturah Shittu, a female millionaire carpenter was commissioned to design the Lagos state deputy governor’s conference room.

WELDING

Nsukka welders ought to be given an opportunity to explore the world of welding in a different way. Do you know that the skill welding and fabrication does not just end in construction of gates and doors, it also includes manufacturing and repairing important metallic machines like welding of planes, trains and many more. Our welders could go into production and coupling too.

UPHOLSTERER

Providing furnitures especially seats with padding springs, webbing and fabric or leather covers, makes the skill a tasking one. A little creativity or innovation most times makes the difference. Nsukka people and the world at large wants to see the wonders you can do to furnitures with your skill, what makes you stand out.

FASHION DESIGNING

As the world evolves so does everything in it evolves, the fashion industry has been set on fire with the current evolution. We have passed the stage of just cutting fabrics and sewing them together, it is now all about creativity

FABRICATION

Janko Peric and Jan Marijnissen are notable welders who made a living from welding. Nsukka welders ought to be given an opportunity to explore the world of welding in a different way. Take it upon yourself and register for NSUKKA VOCATIONAL SKILL AND TALENT COMPETITION 2021 to show the world what you can do from your workshop as there would be platform for innovations and creativity.

PHOTOGRAPHER

Zhang Jinga(The Chinese Photographer ) and Timothy Allen has proven a lot of people who believe that photographers don’t do well in the financial department of life. Nsukka Vocational Skill and Talent 2021 gives you the platform to prove to the world that there is more to photography than assumed.

SINGERS

One of the major entertainers of the society are the singers, the likes of Davido, Celine Dione and some other musicians. Nsukka Vocational Skill and Talent Competition 2021 gives you the platform to be celebrated like these popular singers

BAKERS

Show the world what you can do with your bakery, Duff Goldman, Anna Olson, Buddy Valastro and many more have written their name in gold just because of the wonders they did with their oven. We offer you a platform at Nsukka Vocational Skill and Talent Competition 2021 to show us what you can do, how creative you can get with your bakeries.

COMEDY STARS

Rowan Sebastian Atkinson(Mr. bean) and even our own Bright Okpocha (Basketmouth) have written their names in gold by just making people laugh. Nsukka Vocational Skill and Talent Competition 2021 gives you a platform to crack peoples rib and leave your legacy. We encourage creativity.

CREATIVE ARTIST

A creative artist is not just a painter or musician or writer, he/she is someone who sees the world a little different than others. Can you influence People through your innate gift and talents? Do you think outside the box? The world cannot forget Pablo Picasso in a hurry even after so many decades of death.

SHOE MAKERS/COBBLERS

Psychologist actually observed that when people approach you the first thing they take notice of, is your foot wear. That makes the shoemakers/cobblers so relevant to mankind. Companies like John lobb, Prada, Balenciaga have written their names in gold by just making shoes.

BARBERS

The world of barbing is a very lucrative and rewarding venture, the likes of Ramesh Babu have made millions from just styling and cutting people’s hair. The world craves creativity and innovation, if you are a barber and has what it takes to make heads turn when they see your work, Nsukka Vocational Skill and Talent Competition 2021 it’s not just the right platform for you but also the ultimate one.

BEAD MAKING

Bead-making does not just end in the attachment of bead to one another by stringing them with sewing needle or a beading needle and thread or wire, most persons can do that. At Nsukka Vocational Skill And Talent Competition 2021 we want to see what makes your work stand out, we want to see what you do that has not been seen before, the creativity behind your work.

Let us know your Ideas

Physically Challenged?

Discover your Talents

We are here for you, look no more; your talent is right on the inside of you, all you need is people like us to assist you and your star will shine.
